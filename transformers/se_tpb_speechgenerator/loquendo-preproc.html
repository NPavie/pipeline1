Years (dmfc/transformers/se_tpb_speechgenerator/config/year_en.xml)

Decades no longer pronounced with hundred
1950s » nineteen hundred fifties » nineteen fifties
21th century as "two thousand" instead of "twenty hundred".

Name initials

D.G.Pritchard
The problem with sentence boundary after initials is solved by adding "D.", "G." etc. in the abbreviation file.

Roman numerals

I-XX as cardinals when preceded by words as "section", "part", "paper", etc. As ordinals if following other words with capital initial.

Longer roman numerals, e.g. MLCCCVIX are spelled out instead of read as one word.

Miscellaneous

Three-level headline numbers (e.g. "3.1.2"):
No longer a pause between the last two numbers.
Big numbers with chunks of three digits ending in "000" (e.g. "1 000 000") now read as a number:

"one million", instead of "one o o o …"
